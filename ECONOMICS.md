# Economics — SpendScan AI

## What's a Converted Lead Worth to Credex?

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. Assumptions:
- Average deal: $500/mo in credits (conservative for a 5–25 person team)
- Gross margin on credits: ~15% (buying at discount, reselling with markup)
- Average customer lifetime: 12 months
- LTV per customer = $500 × 12 × 0.15 = **$900**

For enterprise deals (larger teams, $2k+/mo): LTV = $2,000 × 12 × 0.15 = **$3,600**

Using blended estimate: **LTV ≈ $1,200** (mix of small and mid-market)

## CAC by Channel

| Channel | Estimated Reach | Conversion to Audit | Audit → Lead | Lead → Customer | CAC |
|---------|----------------|---------------------|--------------|-----------------|-----|
| HN front page | 5,000 clicks | 40% = 2,000 audits | 25% = 500 leads | 2% = 10 customers | $0 |
| Reddit organic | 300 clicks/post | 35% = 105 audits | 25% = 26 leads | 2% = 0.5 customers | $0 |
| Newsletter feature | 2,000 clicks | 30% = 600 audits | 25% = 150 leads | 2% = 3 customers | $0 |
| VC portfolio email | 400 opens | 60% = 240 audits | 35% = 84 leads | 5% = 4 customers | $0 |

All channels above are $0 paid budget in month 1. At scale, if Credex runs paid:
- LinkedIn (targeting EM/CTO): ~$80 CPC, 3% audit completion, 25% lead rate, 2% close = **CAC ~$5,300** (not viable at current LTV)
- Paid is not the play here. This is an organic, content-driven lead-gen tool.

## Conversion Funnel

```
Landing page visit       → 1,000
Audit started            → 400 (40%)
Audit completed          → 320 (80% of started)
Email captured           → 96 (30% of completed)
Credex consultation booked → 12 (12.5% of emails, only high-savings cases)
Credit purchase          → 3 (25% of consultations)
```

At $1,200 LTV and 3 customers per 1,000 visitors: **Revenue per 1,000 visits = $3,600**

This is a strong content-tool economics model. SEO-driven tools in adjacent spaces (payroll calculators, marketing ROI tools) see $2,000–$8,000 per 1,000 organic visits.

## $1M ARR in 18 Months — What Would Have to Be True

- **ARR = $1M** → need ~833 active credit customers at $100/mo revenue each
- At 25% close rate from consultation: need 3,332 consultations booked
- At 12.5% consultation rate from email leads: need 26,666 email leads
- At 30% email capture rate: need **88,888 audit completions**
- Over 18 months: ~4,938 audits/month needed by month 18

Realistic growth path:
- Month 1: 500 audits (HN launch, Reddit)
- Month 6: 3,000 audits/month (newsletter features, referral flywheel, SEO starting to kick in)
- Month 12: 8,000 audits/month (SEO for "AI tool pricing" queries, VC partnerships, referral codes)
- Month 18: 12,000 audits/month (established brand, Google ranking for tool comparison queries)

Cumulative: ~120,000 audits → ~36,000 leads → ~4,500 consultations → ~1,125 customers → $1.35M ARR

**This is achievable if:** The HN/Product Hunt launch generates an SEO backlink anchor, the referral loop works (shareable results drive 0.3 referrals per completed audit), and Credex closes consultations at 25%+ (which requires a strong sales motion).

**Pivot trigger:** If consultation-to-close rate drops below 10% after 50 consultations, the audit is attracting the wrong users (too small, not actually buying credits). Tighten the savings threshold for surfacing Credex (raise from $500/mo to $1,000/mo).
